PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to DIGID FLY. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game. By playing DIGID FLY, you agree to the practices described here.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

DIGID FLY is a browser-based game. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Game Data: Your player name, high score, completed stages, and unlocked characters are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Usage Data via Ads: If you see advertisements (via Google AdSense), Google may collect anonymized usage data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited to display relevant ads.
  • Cookies: Google AdSense may use cookies to serve personalized advertisements. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

2. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

  • Local game data is used solely to save your progress and provide a better gaming experience.
  • Ad-related data is processed by Google AdSense under their privacy policy to display relevant advertisements.
  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

3. GOOGLE ADSENSE

DIGID FLY uses Google AdSense to display advertisements to non-subscribed users. Google AdSense:

  • May use cookies and web beacons to track interactions with ads.
  • May collect data to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history.
  • Operates under Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads.

4. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

DIGID FLY is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete such information.

5. DATA SECURITY

All game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit game data to external servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect any data that passes through our systems.

6. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our game may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local game data by clearing your browser's localStorage.
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google's settings.
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the game constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. CONTACT

For privacy-related questions, please contact us via the GitHub repository for DIGID FLY.
